Youth
Representing the Club in the Eastern Junior Alliance and FA Youth Cup!

For the forthcoming 2020/2021 season Braintree Town Football Club have decided to introduce their own Elite Youth Section with an Under 18s side participating in the Easter Junior Alliance. The Under 18's will also represent the Club in the FA Youth Cup!

The Under 18s will be playing on the First Team training pitch at Cressing Road, with selected mid-week cup matches under floodlights.

Lots more news on teams and fixtures will appear here over the coming weeks and months - watch this space!

All team players will be full members of the Club and will receive a season ticket to all first team matches in the Vanarama National League at the Cressing Road Stadium.

Want to get involved? Yes, then e-mail the club at: info@braintreetownfc.org.uk.

Child safety is a primary concern to the Club. All managers, coaches and staff will be subject to FA Criminal Record Check (CRC) every three years and meet The FA's Safeguarding Children policy. The Club and, in particular, Club Welfare Officer will ensure we:

- Only involve suitable people.
- Create a safe environment for all.
- Recognise and report any concerns.

Our aim is to create a safe and fun environment for children and young people to promote best practice throughout the Club and to enjoy 'The Great Game'!
