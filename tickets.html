Payment
Payment for season tickets can be made via card, cash or cheque. Please make cheques payable to 'Braintree Town Football Club Limited'.

Tickets will be available for collection from the ground on the Saturday before the season starts.

If you wish to have your season ticket posted to you, please provide a stamped addressed envelope.
